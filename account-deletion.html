IronFist Privacy

Request account deletion

Use this page to request deletion of your IronFist account and associated cloud data. If you can still sign in, you can also use the in-app account deletion control, but email is the safest path if you want the full request recorded and processed.

Email deletion request support@codedominion.com

Response target

Within 30 days

Request channel

Registered email or in-app request

Support contact

support@codedominion.com

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to support@codedominion.com from the email address linked to your IronFist account.
  2. Use the subject line IronFist Account Deletion Request.
  3. Include any details that help us identify the account, such as your display name, platform, and the email address you used to register.

What data this request covers

  • Account profile and authentication-linked cloud records
  • Synced training history, progress, favorites, decks, and camp data
  • Public leaderboard/profile entries associated with your account

Important notes

  • Camera video is processed locally on device. IronFist does not store uploaded camera recordings as part of normal app use.
  • If you used IronFist only in guest mode, there may be no cloud account to delete. In that case, clearing app storage or uninstalling the app removes local-only data from your device.
  • Some limited records may be retained where required to comply with law, resolve disputes, or protect the service from abuse.
